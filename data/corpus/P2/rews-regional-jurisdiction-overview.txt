REWS Regional Jurisdiction Overview — East Asia biobanks and legal bases
Source: GA4GH REWS regional scope (RegBot Phase 1 corpus index)
Tier: P2 | Jurisdiction: INTL | Framework: REWS

DISCLAIMER: Index for RegBot retrieval only; not legal advice. Pair with per-region excerpt files.

Singapore (SG)
National programmes: National Precision Medicine (SG10K, PRECISE SG100K, SG INSIGHT).
Legal basis: Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA); Human Biomedical Research Act (HBRA); Health Information Bill (HIB).
See: data/corpus/P2/SG/pdpa-hbra-excerpt.txt

Mainland China (CN)
Programmes: China Kadoorie Biobank, China National GeneBank, NGDC, precision medicine initiatives.
Legal basis: Human Genetic Resources Regulation; PIPL; Data Security Law; HGR Management Measures.
See: data/corpus/P2/CN/hgr-pipl-excerpt.txt

Taiwan (TW)
Programmes: Taiwan Biobank, Taiwan Precision Medicine Initiative (TPMI), NBCT.
Legal basis: Human Biobank Management Act; Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).
See: data/corpus/P2/TW/biobank-pdpa-excerpt.txt

South Korea (KR)
Programmes: National Biobank of Korea, KOBIC/K-BDS, BIKO, K-gNBS.
Legal basis: Bioethics and Safety Act; Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA); AI Framework Act (2025/26).
See: data/corpus/P2/KR/bioethics-pipa-excerpt.txt

Japan (JP)
Programmes: Biobank Japan, Tohoku Megabank, NCBN, Genomic Medicine Japan (GeMJ).
Legal basis: Ethical Guidelines for Medical and Health Research Involving Human Subjects; APPI; Act on Promotion of Genomic Medicine (2023).
See: data/corpus/P2/JP/ethics-appi-excerpt.txt

Hong Kong (HK)
Programmes: Hong Kong Genome Project.
Legal basis: Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO); cross-border transfer Section 33 (not yet in force); PCPD model clauses.
See: data/corpus/P2/HK/pdpo-excerpt.txt

Cross-border review checklist: identify source jurisdiction, consent scope, transfer mechanism, ethics/IRB pathway, and whether genetic/health data triggers special-category rules in destination country.
